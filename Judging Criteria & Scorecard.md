# Create Apps Championship: Qualifications Stage
**Initiative by:** Dubai Chamber Digital  
**Document:** Judging Criteria & Scorecard

## Scoring Overview
All categories are evaluated on a **Scale of 1 to 5**:
*   **1** - Minimal expectations met
*   **5** - Surpassed all their expectations

---

## 1. Ideation Category

| Categories | Sub-Category | Scale | Detailed Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Ideation** | **Problem & Impact** | 1 - 5 | • Participants clearly identify problems, solutions & rewards.<br>• The Unique Value Proposition is clearly stated.<br>• The ideal customer persona is clearly identified. |
| **Ideation** | **Originality** | 1 - 5 | • The App offers a new solution or applies existing solutions in a new, innovative way. |

---

## 2. Business Category

| Categories | Sub-Category | Scale | Detailed Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Business** | **Viability** | 1 - 5 | • The pitch clearly identifies market strategy and includes some user feedback or validation, as well as the market potential.<br>• The technological and operational aspects of the solution is realistic and feasible.<br>• The App idea has the potential to be developed and established in Dubai/UAE and/Or Scale Globally. |
| **Business** | **Business Model** | 1 - 5 | • The pitch includes mention of the monetization strategy, for example the revenue model and pricing strategy is clearly identified.<br>• The pitch situates the App within the context of Dubai's Innovative Business Ecosystem. |

---

## 3. Technical Category

| Categories | Sub-Category | Scale | Detailed Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Technical** | **Execution** | 1 - 5 | • The Demo Prototype of the Mobile App feels clear, intuitive, and easy to follow.<br>• The prototype is easy to navigate and understand for the intended audience.<br>• Core features are demonstrated as expected, with smooth transitions and logical workflows. |
| **Technical** | **Feasibility** | 1 - 5 | • The demo prototype represents a realistic and achievable solution using the current resources.<br>• The app demonstrates the potential for scalability and future enhancements. |
| **Technical** | **Design** | 1 - 5 | • The demo interface is visually appealing and consistent with the app's branding.<br>• The design provides a positive and intuitive user experience.<br>• The design reflects the brand's identity and includes accessibility considerations. |

---

## 4. Pitch Category

| Categories | Sub-Category | Scale | Detailed Criteria |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Pitch Deck** | **Content and Clarity** | 1 - 5 | • Does the pitch deck effectively communicate the app's concept, value proposition, and goals in a concise and structured way? |
| **Pitch Deck** | **Visual Appeal** | 1 - 5 | • Are the slides visually engaging, with consistent branding, professional design, and appropriate use of visuals? |
| **Pitch Deck** | **Data and Validation** | 1 - 5 | • Does the pitch deck include relevant data or evidence (e.g., market research, user feedback) to support the app's potential? |
| **Pitch Skills** | **Delivery and Communication** | 1 - 5 | • Is the presenter visible, confident, articulate, and engaging in their delivery, with a clear and impactful message? |
